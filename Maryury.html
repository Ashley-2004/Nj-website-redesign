Maryury A. Martinetti

Department of Revenue and Finance

Commissioner Maryury Martinetti has been a Commissioner since 2004 and has worked to stabilize taxes for residents. Moreover, Maryury is a trustee for the ARC of New Jersey, which is committed to enhancing the quality of life of children and adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities and their families. Maryury resides in town with her husband Marty and two children.
