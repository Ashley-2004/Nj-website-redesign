NEWS:
 1st Quarter Taxes Due February 18th / Abatements Due February 10

Please be advised that taxes are due on February 18, 2020 and 1st quarter abatements are due February 10, 2020.
The Tax Office will be open until 8 p.m. on Tuesday, February 18, 2020.
We will be closed on February 12th and February 17th.


Important Tax Information:

The federal government recently adopted a new tax plan that includes a combined deduction limit on property taxes of $10,000 to begin in 2018. Due to this change, property owners have been inquiring about pre-paying property taxes in 2017 for 2018.

The City of Union City will accept pre-payments of 2018 taxes in 2017 and, as a convenience to property owners, the Tax Collector's Office will remain open until 6 PM on December 29, 2017. Property owners may also pay online through December 31, 2017. Credit Cards can be used online, but can not be accepted in person at the office.

Property owners may estimate their tax bill based on the 2017 bill. If changes occur to individual bills based on tax rate changes or revaluations, you will either have a credit or balance in 2018.

The City of Union City cannot offer you advice concerning your pre-payment of taxes and individuals should consult their tax advisors. The information and extended hours are provided as a courtesy to property owners.
